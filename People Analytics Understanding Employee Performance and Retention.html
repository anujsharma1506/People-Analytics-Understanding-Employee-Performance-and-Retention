plt.ylabel("Count")
plt.show()

Findings:

  1. Majority "Fully Meets":
    • A large majority of employees (~2,000) have a performance score of "Fully Meets," indicating satisfactory performance across the workforce.
  2. Few Low Scores:
    • Relatively few employees are categorized as "Needs Improvement" or on a "Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)."

Insights:

  1. High Performance Levels:
    • The predominance of "Fully Meets" scores reflects a generally competent and effective workforce, suggesting strong hiring practices or effective management.
  2. Focus on Outliers:
    • Efforts can be directed at employees with "Needs Improvement" or "PIP" scores through training, mentoring, or re-evaluating performance metrics to boost overall productivity.
